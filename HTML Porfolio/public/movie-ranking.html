The Best Movies According to me!

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar 2014

Interstellar follows astronauts seeking a new home for humanity through a wormhole, exploring time, love, and survival.

Naruto

Best anime of all time! Naruto follows Naruto Uzumaki, an outcast ninja striving to become Hokage while overcoming challenges and discovering his potential.

Troy 2004

Troy depicts the epic battle between Greece and Troy, driven by love, honor, and betrayal.
